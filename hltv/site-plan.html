Site Plan for Counter-Strike Portal

1. Home Page

Usability: Introduces the site, shares a personal story, and highlights the benefits of gaming. It aims to attract users to explore more about Counter-Strike.

2. Browse Teams Page

Usability: Displays a list of teams, allowing users to view detailed information about each. It facilitates the search for specific teams and encourages interaction.

3. Browse Players Page

Usability: Provides information about players, their statistics, and performance. Helps users get to know their favorite players better and follow their careers.

4. Browse Matches Page

Usability: Lists upcoming matches and past results. Allows users to stay updated on the competition schedule.

5. Match Details Page

Usability: Provides detailed information about a specific match, including teams, scores, and statistics. Helps users dive deeper into the details of each game.

6. Team Profile/Detail Page

Usability: Displays detailed information about a specific team, including player rosters, team history, and achievements. It enhances user engagement with the team's narrative and stats.

7. Player Profile/Detail Page

Usability: Showcases individual player profiles with stats, match history, and personal achievements. This helps fans connect with players and track their performance.
